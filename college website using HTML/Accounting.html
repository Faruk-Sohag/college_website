Accounting

Accounting is the process of systematically and methodically recording, analyzing, and summarizing financial transactions of an organization or individual. It plays a fundamental role in providing crucial information for decision-making, financial reporting, and ensuring the accountability of financial resources

Accounting is a critical aspect of modern business and finance. It not only provides financial information for decision-making but also serves as a tool for transparency, accountability, and the assessment of an entity's financial health. Various careers are associated with accounting, including certified public accountants (CPAs), management accountants, auditors, and financial analysts. Students studying accounting gain a strong foundation in financial principles and are equipped with skills that are highly relevant in various industries and organizations.
